⚡ How to Play SudoSpark

Fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with numbers 1–9 without repeating any number.

🖱️ Click a cell to select it, then click a number or press 1–9 on your keyboard.

📝 Toggle Notes mode to pencil in candidates. Press N to toggle.

💡 Use Hints when you're stuck — you get 3 per game.

🔥 Build your streak by entering correct numbers consecutively!

⭐ Earn score points — harder puzzles and fewer mistakes = higher score.

💔 You have 3 mistakes max. Be careful!

Shortcuts: Delete/Backspace to erase, H for hint, Z to undo.
